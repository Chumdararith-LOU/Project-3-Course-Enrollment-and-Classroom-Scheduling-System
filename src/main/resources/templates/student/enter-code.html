Enter Enrollment Code

Use the enrollment code provided by your instructor or administrator to register for a course.

Enrollment codes are typically 8-16 characters long and case-sensitive.

How to get enrollment codes:

  • • Contact your course instructor
  • • Check your student portal announcements
  • • Visit the administrative office
  • • Check your email for course invitations

Important Notes:

  • • Make sure you meet all course prerequisites before enrolling
  • • Enrollment codes may have expiration dates
  • • Some courses have limited capacity and may fill up quickly
  • • You can drop courses within the specified deadline period
